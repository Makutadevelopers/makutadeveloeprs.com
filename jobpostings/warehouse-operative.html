Warehouse Operative (m/f/d) – Full Time
Location: Germany, Neusorg  |  Type: Full Time

Job Description

As a Warehouse Operative at Makuta Developers, you will be responsible for the efficient receipt, storage, and dispatch of a wide range of goods. You will play a key role in our supply chain process, ensuring our products reach our customers in perfect condition.

Your Responsibilities

  • Receiving, moving, checking, and storing incoming goods
  • Picking and packing orders for dispatch
  • Ensuring stock is stored safely and accurately
  • Maintaining accurate stock records and documentation
  • Operating warehouse equipment safely
  • Keeping the warehouse clean and organized

Your Profile

  • Experience in warehouse operations preferred
  • Good organizational and time management skills
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Attention to detail and reliability
  • Basic computer skills

We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits
  • Modern working environment
  • Opportunities for professional growth
  • Supportive team culture
