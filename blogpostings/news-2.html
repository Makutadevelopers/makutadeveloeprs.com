Makuta hosts annual developer summit

By Makuta Team | 10 August 2025

The 2025 Makuta Developer Summit brought together innovators, engineers, and thought leaders from around the world. The event featured keynote speeches, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities for the global developer community.

  • Keynote by Makuta CEO on the future of AI
  • Breakout sessions on cloud, security, and automation
  • Live demos of new Makuta products

We thank all participants and look forward to next year's summit!
